# Engagement Notes

## Evidence-Only Output
All conclusions are expressed through evidence artifacts.
No verbal or informal verdicts are provided.

## Time-of-Capture
All results reflect system behavior at the time of execution only.
Subsequent changes invalidate prior seals.

## Re-Audits
Re-evaluation requires a new engagement and a newly authorized boundary.

## Communication
Communication is limited to:
- Authorization confirmation
- Boundary clarification
- Delivery notification

Interpretation of results is the responsibility of the client.
