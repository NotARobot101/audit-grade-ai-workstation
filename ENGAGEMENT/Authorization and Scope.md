# Authorization & Scope Declaration

This document defines the conditions under which an EasyStreet / AEGIS-ALD-W1 engagement may proceed.

## Authorization
The client affirms that they are authorized to grant access to the specified evaluation boundary and that such access does not violate internal policy, contractual obligations, or applicable law.

## Scope
Evaluation is strictly limited to the explicitly authorized boundary.
No inference is made beyond the provided artifacts.

## Exclusions
- No evaluation of future behavior
- No evaluation of unobserved systems
- No certification of safety, correctness, or compliance
- No responsibility for deployment decisions

## Independence
EasyStreet operates as an independent evaluator.
Results are produced regardless of outcome.

## Engagement Termination
Any scope ambiguity, missing authorization, or invariant breach results in immediate fail-closed termination of the evaluation.
